Canteens




The Main Canteen



The Main Canteen, located amidst the lush greenery on campus, offers a tranquil dining experience for those seeking a connection with nature. Specializing in organic and locally sourced vegetarian dishes, this canteen is a haven for those who prioritize sustainability and well-being. The menu at Amrita's Main Canteen is a celebration of seasonal produce, with an emphasis on nourishing bowls, smoothies, and guilt-free desserts. The open-air seating allows students to enjoy their meals while surrounded by nature's beauty, making it the perfect spot for a refreshing break between classes. Indulge in their signature green smoothies and nutrient-packed Buddha bowls for a wholesome and satisfying meal.

The MBA Canteen



MBA Canteen is the go-to spot for those seeking a fusion of global flavors in their vegetarian fare. Situated near the academic block, this canteen takes students on a culinary journey with its diverse menu inspired by cuisines from around the world. From aromatic curries to zesty stir-fries, MBA Canteen caters to the adventurous taste buds of the college community. The vibrant and modern setting provides an ideal backdrop for students to relax and savor their meals. Be sure to explore their themed nights, where the menu transforms to showcase the richness of vegetarian cuisines across cultures.











The IT Canteen



Welcome to IT Canteen, the epitome of wholesome and flavorful vegetarian dining on campus. Nestled in the heart of the college, this canteen is a haven for health-conscious students. From fresh salads bursting with colorful veggies to hearty grain bowls and innovative plant-based wraps, IT Canteen offers a diverse menu that caters to every palate. The cozy ambiance, adorned with potted plants and eco-friendly decor, creates a serene atmosphere for students to enjoy their meals. Don't miss their daily specials, crafted with seasonal ingredients to ensure a delightful and sustainable dining experience.
